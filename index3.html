Incentives

The amount of subsidy provided by the Indian government for solar energy projects varies depending on the specific scheme or program. However, I can provide you with some general information on the subsidies provided by the government.

Capital Subsidy

The government provides a capital subsidy of up to 30% of the benchmark cost for solar power projects. The benchmark cost is determined by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) and is periodically updated.

Accelerated depreciation

Businesses can claim accelerated depreciation of up to 40% on solar power projects, which reduces their tax liability.The government provides interest subvention of up to 2% on loans taken for solar power projects.

Subsidies for solar water pumps

The government provides a subsidy of up to 30% of the benchmark cost for solar water pumps.
